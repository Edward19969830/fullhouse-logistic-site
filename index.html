公司简介

About Us

FullHouse Logistic是一家位于美国加利福尼亚州安大略市的专业海外仓，仓库面积40,000平方英尺。我们专注为中国跨境电商卖家及海外品牌商提供本地仓储、一件代发、退货处理、换标等全方位服务，助力客户高效拓展美国市场。

FullHouse Logistic is a professional fulfillment warehouse located in Ontario, California, with a warehouse area of 40,000 square feet. We provide comprehensive services including local warehousing, order fulfillment, returns processing, and relabeling for Chinese cross-border e-commerce sellers and overseas brands, helping clients efficiently expand in the US market.

团队介绍

Team Introduction

FullHouse Logistic核心运营团队拥有10年以上美国本地仓储物流行业经验。从小型仓库起步，实战管理过百万平方英尺级别的大型海外仓，擅长仓库规划、团队建设、流程优化等多环节运营管理，为客户提供高效、可靠的仓储及供应链解决方案。

The core operation team of FullHouse Logistic has over 10 years of experience in the US warehousing and logistics industry. Starting from small-scale warehouses, the team has managed large-scale overseas warehouses of over one million square feet. We specialize in warehouse planning, team building, process optimization, and multi-link operation management, providing clients with efficient and reliable warehousing and supply chain solutions.
